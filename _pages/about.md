I am a 4th-year PhD student in the Department of Social Science at University Carlos III of Madrid. My research sits at the intersection of historical political economy and the study of collective action. I am primarily interested in the historical determinants and legacies of collective action and civil society. My dissertation delves into the causes and (long-term) consequences of labor mobilization in Spain prior to the Second Spanish Republic (1931-1936). I also explore the role of long-run population dynamics in constraining or facilitating historical persistence.
